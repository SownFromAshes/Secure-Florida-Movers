Insurance and Protection

We offer two levels of coverage: Basic coverage at $0.60 per pound (included at no charge) and Full Value Protection for complete replacement value. We recommend Full Value Protection for valuable items. We also carry comprehensive liability insurance for your peace of mind.

If damage occurs, report it immediately and note it on the inventory sheet. File a written claim within 9 months for interstate moves or 6 months for local moves. We investigate all claims promptly and work to resolve them fairly based on your chosen coverage level.

For valuable items, we highly recommend Full Value Protection. If you have particularly valuable items (artwork, antiques, electronics), consider additional coverage. Check with your homeowner's or renter's insurance to see what coverage they provide during moves.

Be ready when we arrive! Pack a survival kit with essentials, ensure pathways are clear, disconnect appliances, and have important documents with you. Remove valuable items like jewelry and cash. Be present during the entire move to answer questions and check inventory.
