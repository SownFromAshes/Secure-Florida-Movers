Understanding Your Coverage Options

Choose the right protection level for your move

Basic Coverage (Released Value)

Included at no additional charge. Provides minimal protection at $0.60 per pound per item. Suitable for items with little monetary value.

Full Value Protection

Comprehensive coverage for replacement value of damaged items. Movers repair, replace, or provide cash settlement for lost or damaged items.

High-Value Items

Special declaration and coverage for items exceeding $100 per pound. Artwork, antiques, and electronics may require additional protection.

Homeowner's Insurance

Check your existing homeowner's or renter's insurance policy. Some policies provide coverage during moves, supplementing our protection.

Claims Process

Report damage immediately upon delivery. We have 30 days to acknowledge claims and 120 days to resolve. Keep all documentation organized.

Coverage Recommendations

We recommend Full Value Protection for moves over $5,000 in value. Consider third-party insurance for extremely valuable collections or artwork.

Filing Claims & Getting Assistance

Step-by-step process for filing insurance claims

Report Damage Immediately

Note any visible damage on the delivery receipt before signing. Take photos of damaged items and contact us within 24 hours to begin the claims process.

Submit Written Claim

File written claims within 9 months for interstate moves or 6 months for local moves. Include photos, receipts, and detailed descriptions of damage.

Claims Resolution

We acknowledge claims within 30 days and resolve within 120 days. Options include repair, replacement, or cash settlement based on your coverage level.
