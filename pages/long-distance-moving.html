Long-Distance Moving FAQs

Your most pressing questions, answered with clarity.

How is a long-distance move priced?

Unlike local moves which are hourly, we price long-distance moves on a flat-rate basis. The cost is determined by the volume (cubic feet) of your belongings and the distance of the move. This allows us to provide a guaranteed, binding quote that will not change.

Will my belongings be on a truck with other people's stuff?

Never. This is our core promise. When you book with us, you are booking the entire truck for your exclusive use. This is the single most important factor in preventing lost items and ensuring a fast, direct delivery.

How long does a long-distance move take?

Because we use dedicated trucks, delivery is much faster than with large van lines. A move within Florida often takes just 1-2 days. Cross-country moves can range from 3 to 7 days, depending on the distance. We will provide a precise delivery window in your quote.

Is everything insured?

Yes. We are fully licensed by the DOT for interstate moves (License # pending) and carry comprehensive insurance. Your quote will include the federally mandated basic liability coverage, and we offer more extensive Full Value Protection plans for complete peace of mind.
