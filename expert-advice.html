Moving Myths Debunked

Separating fact from fiction in the moving industry

1

Myth: All Moving Companies Are the Same

Reality: Moving companies vary greatly in licensing, insurance, experience, and service quality. Always research credentials, read reviews, and verify licensing before hiring.

2

Myth: Estimates Should Always Be Free

Reality: While many companies offer free estimates, quality in-home estimates take time and expertise. Professional estimates that cost a small fee often provide more accurate pricing.

3

Myth: Pack Everything Yourself to Save Money

Reality: While DIY packing saves labor costs, professional packing often prevents damage that costs more than the service. Consider professional packing for fragile items.

4

Myth: Moving Insurance Covers Everything

Reality: Basic coverage is minimal ($0.60/lb). Full Value Protection provides better coverage but has limitations. Read the fine print and consider additional insurance for valuables.

5

Myth: Summer is Always the Most Expensive

Reality: While summer is peak season, specific dates matter more than seasons. End-of-month, weekends, and holidays are more expensive regardless of season.

Professional Moving Secrets

Before You Move

  • 📋 Get 3 written estimates from licensed companies
  • 📅 Book movers 4-6 weeks in advance
  • 📦 Start decluttering 8 weeks before moving
  • 📋 Create a moving binder for all documents
  • 💰 Set aside cash for moving day tips

Packing Like a Pro

  • 📦 Use small boxes for heavy items
  • 🗞️ Pack plates vertically like records
  • 👕 Leave clothes in dressers for local moves
  • 🔗 Take photos of electronic setups
  • 🏷️ Label boxes on multiple sides

Moving Day Success

  • ☕ Have coffee and snacks ready for the crew
  • 📱 Keep important numbers easily accessible
  • 🚿 Do a final walkthrough before leaving
  • 📝 Read all paperwork before signing
  • 💡 Keep a toolbox handy for immediate needs

After the Move

  • 📋 Check inventory lists carefully
  • 📊 Report any damage within 24 hours
  • ⭐ Leave honest reviews for your movers
  • 📮 Update voter registration and licenses
  • 🏠 Register with local utilities and services
